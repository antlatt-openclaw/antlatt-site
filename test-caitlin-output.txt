Intel N100 mini PCs are excellent for homelabs due to their low power consumption (typically 6W TDP), making them ideal for 24/7 home server workloads without driving up electricity bills. They offer surprisingly capable performance for lightweight services like Pi-hole, Home Assistant, Docker containers, and media servers, while staying whisper-quiet and compact. With modern features like AV1 decode, multiple NVMe slots, and affordable pricing under $200, they're a budget-friendly entry point for anyone building out a home infrastructure.
